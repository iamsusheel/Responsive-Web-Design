Srinivasa Ramanujan Aiyangar

The man who knew infinity

Here's a time line of Srinivasa Ramanujan Aiyangar:
  • 1887 - Born in Erode , Mysore State, British India

  • 1897 - He passed his primary examination in English , Tamil , geography and arithmetic with the best scores in the district.

  • 1909 - Ramanujan married janakia girl his mother had selected for him .

  • 1910 - To make money , he tutored students at presidency college who were preparing for their fellow of arts exam.

  • 1913 - Upon seuring a research position at Madras University , Ramanujan moved with his family to Triplicane.

  • 1916 - His work on highly composite numbers , sections of the first part of which had been published the preceding year in the proceedings of the london mathematical society

  • 1918 - He was the first indian to be elected a fellow of Trinity College , Cambridge

  • 1920 - Dies at the age of 32
